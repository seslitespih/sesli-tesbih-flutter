Privacy Policy

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data.

Vocal Tasbeeh uses the following device features:

  • Microphone & Speech Recognition — Used only to detect dhikr words in real time. Audio is processed entirely on your device and is never recorded or transmitted.
  • Location — Used only to calculate prayer times and Qibla direction. Your location is never stored or shared.

All app data (settings, custom dhikrs, language preference) is stored locally on your device only.

Features

  • Voice-activated dhikr counting — no tapping needed
  • 21 built-in dhikrs + custom dhikr support
  • Accurate GPS-based prayer times
  • Real-time Qibla compass
  • 23 prayers and surahs with translations
  • Available in Turkish, English, and Arabic
